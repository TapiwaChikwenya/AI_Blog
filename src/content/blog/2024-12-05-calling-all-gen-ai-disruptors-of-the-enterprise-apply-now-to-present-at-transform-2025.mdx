# Calling all gen AI disruptors of the enterprise! Apply now to present at Transform 2025

<hr/>
</div><p>The Innovation Showcase is back at <a href="https://venturebeat.com/events/transform-2025/">Transform: The Orchestration of Enterprise Agentic AI at Scale</a>, in June 2025 in San Francisco.&#xa0;</p>



<p>We&#x2019;re on the hunt for the 10 <a href="https://venturebeat.com/ai/openai-launches-full-o1-model-with-34-reduced-error-rate-debuts-chatgpt-pro/">generative AI</a> products most likely to disrupt the enterprise. If you think your technology fits this bill, we&#x2019;d like to invite you to present its impact on the main stage at Transform.</p>



<div/><p>Those selected to present will do so in front of an invite-only audience of 400 industry decision-makers, and will receive direct feedback from a panel of enterprise tech analysts, brand executives and others. Every presenter will receive exclusive editorial coverage from VentureBeat, getting your company out in front of our millions of monthly readers.</p>



<p>Who should apply? Dynamic companies with compelling new <a href="https://venturebeat.com/ai/openai-appears-poised-to-launch-chatgpt-pro-subscription-plans-at-200-usd-per-month/">gen AI technologies</a> eager to present on stage. All finalists will be winners (since we&#x2019;re being selective). We will award winners in three categories: most likely to succeed, best technology, and best presentation style.</p>



<p>In total, up to 10 candidates will be selected from what is sure to be a multitude of qualified applicants. Candidates must offer new enterprise AI solutions, and we will select five early-stage companies (seed to early-stage Series A that have received &#x24;50M or less) and five later-stage startups (series A or later companies that have received more than &#x24;50M). If you represent a unit that is part of a mature, large company, please enter as a later stage.</p>



<p>If have a story to tell and an AI product or service that offers up real business results and use cases, please <a href="https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLScweOzKSpfwAeEm2nuBWeKlKZbHvcOkPsGeTxV2jW0aAtmz8Q/viewform?usp=sf_link" target="_blank">submit your application</a> by 5 p.m. PT on March 31, 2025.<br/><br/>Read about the winners from VB Transform 2024: <a href="https://venturebeat.com/ai/sambanova-systems-wins-coolest-technology-award-at-venturebeat-transform-2024/">SambaNova</a>, <a href="https://venturebeat.com/ai/venturebeats-transform-2024-innovation-showcase-instabase-gets-most-likely-to-succeed/">Instabase</a> and <a href="https://venturebeat.com/ai/tabnine-wins-ai-innovation-award-for-best-presentation-at-vb-transform-2024/">Tabnine</a>.</p>
